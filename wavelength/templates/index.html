What the game is

Wavelength World is inspired by the social board game Wavelength. Every prompt lives on a spectrum, like harmless to dangerous or cold to hot, and the challenge is understanding how the broader player base thinks.

Instead of matching one friend at the table, you are trying to read the average opinion of everyone who has answered that clue.

How it works

  • Submit your own honest position on a spectrum to help shape the crowd average.
  • Make a scored guess about where the overall player base will land.
  • Write clues for hidden targets and compete as both a guesser and a clue giver.

Why play

  • Daily and all-time leaderboard races reward accuracy over randomness.
  • Player-written clues keep the catalog growing with fresh social judgment calls.
  • Quick guest access makes it easy to try a round before committing to an account.
